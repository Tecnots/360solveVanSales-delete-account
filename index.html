Delete Account – 360 Solve VanSales

360 Solve VanSales is a company-managed application. User accounts are created and controlled by the respective company administrator.

How to Request Account Deletion

If you would like to delete your account, please contact your company administrator. The administrator will review and process your account deletion request.

Data Deletion

  • User profile information
  • Login credentials
  • App access permissions

Data Retention

Sales transactions, financial records, and inventory data may be retained by the company for legal, accounting, and audit purposes as required by law.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 7–14 working days after approval by the company administrator.
